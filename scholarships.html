Scholarships & Financial Aid

Making quality education accessible to deserving students

Investing in Your Future

At Creative Palms Institute, we believe that financial circumstances should not be a barrier to accessing quality education. Our scholarship and financial aid programs are designed to support talented and deserving students in achieving their educational and career goals.

We offer various types of scholarships and financial assistance based on academic merit, financial need, and special talents. Our goal is to make our programs accessible to students from diverse backgrounds and circumstances.

Available Scholarship Programs

Academic Excellence Scholarships

Awarded to students with outstanding academic achievements in their previous education.

  • Up to 50% tuition waiver
  • Available for both Certificate and Diploma programs
  • Renewable based on continued academic excellence

Eligibility: Students with exceptional grades in P7, S4, or S6 examinations

Financial Need-Based Grants

Provided to students who demonstrate significant financial need and are unable to afford full tuition.

  • 20% to 40% tuition support
  • Consideration for book and materials allowance
  • Available for all program types

Eligibility: Students from disadvantaged backgrounds with proof of financial hardship

Women in Technical Fields Scholarship

Encouraging female participation in traditionally male-dominated technical and vocational fields.

  • 30% tuition waiver
  • Mentorship opportunities
  • Priority for internship placements

Eligibility: Female students enrolling in technical programs such as Motor Vehicle Mechanics, Electrical Installation, or ICT

Community Service Scholarships

Recognizing students who have made significant contributions to their communities.

  • 25% tuition reduction
  • Leadership development opportunities
  • Community engagement projects

Eligibility: Students with documented history of community service and volunteer work

How to Apply for Scholarships

1

Check Eligibility

Review the eligibility criteria for each scholarship program to determine which ones you qualify for.

2

Prepare Documentation

Gather all required documents, including academic transcripts, financial information, recommendation letters, and personal statement.

3

Complete Application Form

Fill out the scholarship application form, which can be obtained from our Admissions Office or downloaded from the website.

4

Submit Application

Submit your completed application with all supporting documents before the specified deadline.

5

Interview Process

Shortlisted candidates may be invited for an interview with the Scholarship Committee.

Flexible Payment Plans

In addition to scholarships, we offer flexible payment plans to help make education more affordable:

Installment Plan

Spread your tuition payments across the semester with our interest-free installment plans

Work-Study Program

Earn while you learn through part-time work opportunities at the institute

Student Loans

Access low-interest education loans through our partner financial institutions
