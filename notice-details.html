Important April 17, 2025 Admissions Office

Government National Merit Admission

The government has released the national merit admission list for the 2025/2026 academic year. All students who applied for the national merit scholarship should check the list at the admissions office.

Important Dates

  • List Publication Date: April 17, 2025
  • Confirmation Deadline: May 15, 2025
  • Document Submission Deadline: May 30, 2025
  • Orientation Week: August 10-14, 2025

Required Documents

Students who find their names on the merit list are required to submit the following documents for verification:

  1. Original academic certificates and transcripts
  2. National ID or passport
  3. Four passport-sized photographs
  4. Letter of recommendation from previous school
  5. Medical examination report

Scholarship Coverage

The government merit scholarship covers the following:

  • Full tuition fees for the duration of the program
  • Monthly stipend for living expenses
  • Book allowance per semester
  • Research allowance (for final year students)

Important Note

Failure to confirm admission and submit the required documents by the stated deadline will result in the forfeiture of the scholarship. The slot will be allocated to another deserving student on the waiting list.

Contact Information

For any inquiries regarding the national merit admission, please contact:

  • Office: Admissions Office, Room 010, Administration Block
  • Phone: +256 772 345 678
  • Email: admissions@creativepalms.ac.ug
  • Office Hours: Monday to Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM
