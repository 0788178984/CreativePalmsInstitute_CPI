Application Process

Thank you for your interest in joining Creative Palms Business & Vocational Institute. Our application process is designed to be straightforward and supportive. Follow the steps below to begin your journey with us.

1

Choose Your Program

Select from our National Certificate Programs, National Diploma Programs, Short Courses, or Vocational Courses based on your interests and career goals.

2

Complete Application Form

Fill out the application form below with accurate personal and academic information.

3

Submit Supporting Documents

Prepare and upload required documents, including academic certificates, identification, and passport-sized photographs.

4

Application Review

Our admissions team will review your application and contact you regarding your application status.

5

Admission Decision

Receive your admission decision and, if accepted, follow the instructions for enrollment and fee payment.
