National Diploma Programs

2-Year advanced diploma programs designed to prepare you for leadership roles in your industry

Program Overview

Our National Diploma Programs offer advanced professional education over a 2-year period. These programs build on foundational knowledge and develop specialized expertise, preparing students for supervisory and management positions in their chosen field.

Each program combines theoretical study with practical application, ensuring graduates are job-ready with both knowledge and skills demanded by employers. National Diplomas are highly respected qualifications that open doors to career advancement.

Duration

2 Years

Qualification

National Diploma

Entry Requirements

S4 or S6 leavers, Certificate holders

Learning Mode

Full-time, on-campus
