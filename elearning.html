Your Virtual Learning Environment

Welcome to the Creative Palms E-Learning Portal, a comprehensive virtual learning environment designed to enhance your educational experience through digital resources, online collaboration, and flexible learning options.

Our e-learning system provides students and faculty with a robust platform for course delivery, resource sharing, assessment, and communication - accessible anytime, anywhere.

How to Access E-Learning

1

Visit the Portal

Go to https://learn.creativepalms.ac.ug or click the "E-Learning" link in the top navigation menu of our website.

2

Login with Your Credentials

Use your student ID number as your username and your default password (provided during orientation) to log in. First-time users will be prompted to change their password.

3

Access Your Courses

Once logged in, you will see your dashboard with all your enrolled courses. Click on any course to access its materials and activities.

4

Explore Available Resources

Navigate through course modules, assignments, forums, and other learning resources using the intuitive menu system.

E-Learning Support

Frequently Asked Questions

First, ensure you're using the correct username (your student ID) and password. If you've forgotten your password, use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. If you still encounter issues, contact the E-Learning Support team at elearning@creativepalms.ac.ug.

Course visibility depends on your enrollment status and whether the instructor has made the course available. If you believe a course is missing, first check with your instructor to ensure they've published the course. If the issue persists, contact the Registrar's Office to verify your enrollment.

Navigate to the assignment in your course, click on it, and then click the "Submit Assignment" button. You can either type your submission directly or upload a file. Make sure to click "Submit" once you've finished. You can view your submission status and feedback on the same page.
