Entry requirements vary by program. Generally, for Certificate programs, you need a minimum of O-Level with at least 5 passes. For Diploma programs, you need at least 1 principal pass at A-Level or a relevant Certificate. For short courses, there may be minimal or no formal academic requirements. Please visit our Entry Requirements page for specific program requirements.

We have two main intake periods: January/February (Semester 1) and August/September (Semester 2). Applications typically open 3 months before the start of each semester. Short courses have multiple intakes throughout the year. Check our Notice Board for current application deadlines.

You can apply online through our website or in person at our Admissions Office. The application process involves:

  1. Obtaining and filling out an application form
  2. Submitting academic documents
  3. Paying the application fee
  4. Attending an interview (for some programs)

Visit our How to Apply page for detailed information.

Yes, Creative Palms offers several scholarship opportunities for deserving students based on academic excellence, financial need, and special talents. We also have government-sponsored slots for certain programs. For details, visit our Scholarships page.

Creative Palms offers a wide range of programs including:

  • National Certificate Programs (1-2 years)
  • National Diploma Programs (2-3 years)
  • Short Courses (2 weeks to 6 months)
  • Vocational Training Programs

Our programs cover fields such as Business, Information Technology, Tourism & Hospitality, and various vocational skills. See our Courses page for the complete list.

Yes, all our programs are accredited by the National Council for Higher Education (NCHE) and relevant professional bodies. Our National Certificates and Diplomas are recognized throughout East Africa and beyond. Many of our graduates have successfully pursued further studies at universities worldwide or secured employment in international organizations.

English is the primary language of instruction for all our programs. Students are expected to have a good command of English for both written and verbal communication.

Our academic year is divided into two semesters:

  • Semester 1: January/February to May/June
  • Semester 2: August/September to December

Each semester lasts about 17 weeks, including examination periods. Short breaks are provided between semesters and during public holidays.

Tuition fees vary by program. Generally:

  • Certificate Programs: UGX 1,200,000 - 1,500,000 per semester
  • Diploma Programs: UGX 1,500,000 - 1,800,000 per semester
  • Short Courses: UGX 300,000 - 800,000 (total)

For detailed fee structures, please visit our Fees Structure page.

We offer several payment options:

  • Bank deposit/transfer to our institutional accounts
  • Mobile money payments
  • Online payment through our student portal
  • Installment payment plans (upon approval)

All payment details are provided in the admission letter.

Yes, we offer flexible payment plans. Students can pay:

  • Full payment at the beginning of the semester (with a 5% discount)
  • 60% at the beginning of the semester and 40% before mid-semester exams
  • Special installment plans can be arranged with the Finance Office on a case-by-case basis

Yes, there are some additional costs:

  • Registration fee (one-time): UGX 50,000
  • ID card fee: UGX 20,000
  • Development fee: UGX 100,000 per year
  • Examination fee: UGX 150,000 per semester
  • Materials and books (varies by program)
  • Practical training fees (for certain programs)

All additional fees are detailed in the admission letter.

Yes, we have on-campus hostels for both male and female students. Our hostels are secure, well-maintained, and conveniently located within walking distance of academic buildings. Accommodation is provided on a first-come, first-served basis, so early application is recommended. We also have partnerships with off-campus accommodation providers for students who prefer alternative housing options.

Creative Palms has modern computing facilities including:

  • Multiple computer laboratories with high-speed internet
  • Specialized software for different programs
  • Campus-wide Wi-Fi access
  • Printing and scanning services
  • Technical support staff

Our library provides comprehensive resources:

  • Extensive collection of textbooks, reference materials, and journals
  • E-library with access to academic databases
  • Quiet study spaces and group discussion rooms
  • Trained librarians to assist with research
  • Extended opening hours during examination periods

For more information, visit our Library page.

Yes, we have a range of sports and recreational facilities:

  • Multi-purpose sports field (football, athletics)
  • Basketball and volleyball courts
  • Indoor games (chess, table tennis, pool)
  • Student recreation center
  • Outdoor relaxation areas

We also have active sports teams that participate in inter-collegiate competitions.
