Welcome to Creative Palms Business & Vocational Institute

Creative Palms Business & Vocational Institute is a premier educational institution dedicated to providing quality education and practical skills training to students in Uganda.

Established in 2010, we have grown to become one of the leading vocational training institutes in the region, with a focus on delivering industry-relevant programs that prepare our students for successful careers.

Our state-of-the-art facilities, experienced faculty, and comprehensive curriculum ensure that our students receive the best possible education and training to excel in their chosen fields.
