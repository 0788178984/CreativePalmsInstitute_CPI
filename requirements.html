General Admission Requirements

Creative Palms Business & Vocational Institute welcomes applications from students who demonstrate academic potential and a commitment to learning.

Certificate Programs

  • Uganda Certificate of Education (UCE) with at least 5 passes
  • Minimum age of 17 years
  • Proficiency in English language
  • Successful completion of admission interview
  • Previous work experience (for mature entry applicants)

Diploma Programs

  • Uganda Advanced Certificate of Education (UACE) with at least 1 principal pass
  • Relevant certificate from a recognized institution
  • Minimum age of 18 years
  • Proficiency in English language
  • Successful completion of admission interview

Required Documents

  • Completed application form
  • Certified copies of academic certificates
  • Copy of National ID or passport
  • Four passport-sized photographs
  • Letters of recommendation (2)
  • Personal statement (300-500 words)
  • Evidence of application fee payment

Application Process

Our application process is straightforward and designed to help you join our community of learners with ease.

1

Complete Application

Fill out the online application form with your personal and academic information.

2

Submit Documents

Upload all required documents through our online portal or deliver them to our admissions office.

3

Attend Interview

Participate in an admission interview (in-person or online) with our faculty members.

4

Receive Admission

Successful applicants will receive an admission letter with further instructions.

Program-Specific Requirements

Some programs may have additional requirements based on the field of study.

Business Programs

  • Credit in Mathematics at UCE level
  • Good communication skills
  • Basic computer literacy
  • Business acumen demonstrated in interview

Technology Programs

  • Credits in Mathematics and Physics at UCE level
  • Basic computer programming knowledge
  • Logical reasoning abilities
  • Technical aptitude demonstrated in interview

Creative Arts Programs

  • Portfolio of creative work
  • Demonstration of artistic abilities
  • Creative thinking skills
  • Interview with department faculty
