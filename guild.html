Your Voice on Campus

The Creative Palms Student Guild is the official representative body for all students at the institute. We work to enhance student welfare, promote academic excellence, and create a vibrant campus community.

Our leadership is elected by students, for students, ensuring that your concerns and ideas are heard by the institute administration.

Guild Activities & Services

Student Welfare

We advocate for better living conditions, affordable meals, health services, and security on campus.

Academic Affairs

We work with faculty to ensure quality education, fair assessment, and support for struggling students.

Sports & Culture

We organize sports competitions, cultural events, talent shows, and other recreational activities.

Clubs & Societies

We support and coordinate various student clubs that cater to different interests and career paths.

Career Development

We organize career fairs, workshops, and networking events to prepare students for the job market.

Community Outreach

We initiate and participate in community service projects to make a positive impact beyond our campus.
