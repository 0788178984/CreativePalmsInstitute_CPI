Welcome to Our Library

The Creative Palms Institute Library serves as the intellectual hub of our institution, providing comprehensive resources and services to support teaching, learning, and research activities.

Our library houses a vast collection of books, journals, e-resources, and multimedia materials covering all academic disciplines offered at the institute, with specialized collections in business, information technology, tourism, and vocational studies.

Library Rules & Guidelines

General Rules

  • Present a valid student/staff ID card to enter the library
  • Maintain silence in the reading areas
  • Mobile phones should be switched to silent mode
  • Food and drinks are not allowed inside the library
  • Handle library materials and equipment with care
  • Return borrowed materials on time to avoid fines
  • Do not remove library materials without proper check-out

Borrowing Guidelines

User Category Max. Items Loan Period
