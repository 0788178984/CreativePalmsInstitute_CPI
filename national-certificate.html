National Certificate Programs

2-Year professional certificate programs designed to equip you with industry-relevant skills

Program Overview

Our National Certificate Programs are comprehensive 2-year courses designed to provide students with both theoretical knowledge and practical skills in their chosen field. These programs are accredited by relevant national bodies and are highly valued by employers across the country.

Upon successful completion, graduates receive a National Certificate that is recognized throughout the industry, opening doors to employment opportunities or further education.

Duration

2 Years

Qualification

National Certificate

Entry Requirements

P7, S4, or S6 leavers

Learning Mode

Full-time, on-campus
