Admission Requirements

National Certificate

  • O-Level Certificate (6 passes)
  • Primary Leaving Certificate
  • 4 Passport Photos
  • National ID/Birth Certificate

National Diploma

  • A-Level Certificate (2 principal passes)
  • O-Level Certificate
  • 4 Passport Photos
  • National ID

Short & Vocational Courses

  • Minimum P.7 Certificate
  • 2 Passport Photos
  • National ID/Birth Certificate

Important Notice

All documents must be original or certified copies. International students may need additional documentation.
